DJPad

News

Dec 20th, 2011 - We're pleased to announced the release of DJPad for the HP Touchpad.

DJPad is a full software DJing solution, featuring:

  • 2 MP3 players with quick seek, scratch, and cue position
  • Auto-normalizing mixer with 3-band cut EQ
  • Simple playlist support (files in subdirectories) and easy import (just copy over USB)

We have a ton of features planned for incremental upgrades coming soon:

  • Headphone-split or 'external mixer' output modes
  • Proper playlist support with ID3 tag support
  • FX (and a better EQ!)
  • Upgraded OpenGL-based GUI
  • Vertical aligned waveform display with 'record scratch' discs to ease beatmatching
